## Content Strategy Notes

1. **Pricing pages convert best** — Every tool with tiered pricing deserves a dedicated pricing page with affiliate links to paid plans
2. **Comparison pages rank for multiple keywords** — "X vs Y" pages can be updated to include new contenders as the market shifts
3. **Update frequency matters** — AI tools change fast; quarterly content refreshes are essential for maintaining rankings
4. **Long-tail wins** — Keywords with year suffixes (2026) have less competition but need annual updates
5. **"Free" modifier increases clicks** — Keywords containing "free" have higher CTR even if commercial intent is slightly lower
6. **Developer-specific modifiers** — Adding "for Python developers", "for VS Code", "for Node.js" etc. reduces competition while maintaining intent
