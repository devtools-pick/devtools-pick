## Key Recommendations

### For Maximum Revenue
1. **Start with recurring programs** — they compound over time. Notion (50%), Resend (25%), Cursor (20-30%), and Linear (20%) offer the best rates.
2. **Layer in high-bounty one-time programs** for quick wins: DigitalOcean ($200), Vultr ($350), Shopify ($150).
3. **Target the "full stack"** — promote complementary tools (e.g., Vercel + Clerk + Supabase + Resend) for multiple revenue streams per reader.

### For Developer Trust
- Focus on tools you actually use or have tested
- Write honest comparisons, not just promotional content
- Include pricing details and real-world use cases
- Disclose affiliate relationships (FTC requirement)

### International Accessibility
- All listed programs accept international affiliates
- Most use PayPal or bank transfers (no US-only restrictions)
- Check individual programs for payout currency options
- Impact, PartnerStack, and direct programs are most common platforms

## Notes

- **Commission rates and terms change frequently.** Verify on signup pages before applying.
- Some programs (Cursor, Figma, GitHub) may require approval for your specific site.
- Cookie duration is important for long sales cycles (developer tools often have 30-90 day evaluation periods).
- Recurring programs at 15%+ are ideal for building sustainable income.
- Consider joining affiliate networks like **Impact**, **PartnerStack**, and **Rewardful** for access to multiple programs.
- Always read the affiliate terms of service — some prohibit coupon sites, paid search, or email marketing.
