ChatGPT remains one of the most popular AI tools in 2026. In this review, we break down everything you need to know — from its core features to its pricing tiers and real-world performance.

## What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a conversational AI assistant built on OpenAI's GPT models. It can answer questions, generate text, write code, analyze images, and much more.

## Key Features

- **Natural Conversation**: Responds fluently in dozens of languages
- **Code Generation**: Writes, debugs, and explains code in 50+ languages
- **Web Browsing**: Can search the internet for up-to-date information
- **Plugins**: Extends functionality with third-party tools
- **Image Generation**: Creates images via DALL·E integration

## Pricing

ChatGPT offers a free tier with basic access, a Plus plan at $20/month for GPT-4 access, and a Pro plan at $200/month for unlimited GPT-4 access and advanced features.

## Who Should Use It?

ChatGPT is ideal for writers, developers, students, and professionals who want a versatile AI assistant. The free tier is great for casual use, while Plus is recommended for power users.

## Final Verdict

ChatGPT sets the bar for AI chatbots. While alternatives exist, its combination of capability, ecosystem, and ease of use make it hard to beat. We give it **5 out of 5 stars**.
